Custom Body Styling (In Progress/Incomplete)

Project Background

This is an ongoing project for my 2010 Altima Coupe. The body itself is in great condition, for 2010, but I have been very interested in learning more about body work. Considering that my car is severely lacking in aftermarket support, I have not been able to find any body modifications that are unique or worth the money, so I figured I would learn more about the subject and see what I can come up with.

Objective(s)

The objective of this project is to design and build a custom body kit for my car, which will greatly benefit my CAD and technical design abilities.

Process

The project is still in the beginning stages. I am currently in the process of trying to get a model of the stock body of the vehicle into CAD so that I can use it as a canvas for design and measurements. I have been working with very rudimentary 3D scanning, as newer iPhone models feature a LiDAR sensor, but I have yet to produce a usable model. I have found purchaseable models online, but of course, that requires an "investment". I am also considering modeling portions of the body from scratch based off of a multitude of measurements. Once I have "prototype" designs, I plan to make them with my 3D printers, starting with PLA for fitment testing. The final designs will have to be printed in much more heat resistent materials such as PETG or ABS, and most likely also reinforced by more automotive friendly materials such as body filler or fiberglass.

Conclusion

In progress.
